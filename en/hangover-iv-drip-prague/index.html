    About Hangover IV Drip Service in Prague

    NIUS provides mobile hangover IV drip therapy in Prague, Czech Republic. A registered nurse travels to your hotel, Airbnb, or home within approximately 60 minutes of booking. The service is available daily from 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM, including weekends and holidays. All treatments are overseen by a prescribing doctor. NIUS serves all Prague districts including Old Town, New Town, Vinohrady, Karlín, Smíchov, Holešovice, and Žižkov. Four hangover IV drip options are available: Nausea Relief (2,850 CZK) with anti-nausea medication and rehydration — the most popular choice for severe hangovers; Pure Hydrate (2,450 CZK) for basic saline rehydration; Electrolyte Reset (2,950 CZK) with 2000mg Magnesium for muscle recovery; and Immunity Lite (2,800 CZK) with Vitamin C for hangover combined with cold symptoms. All prices are all-inclusive with no hidden fees. The infusion takes approximately 60 minutes. Contact: +420 773 629 123 (phone/WhatsApp). Online booking: nurseinprague.setmore.com/book.

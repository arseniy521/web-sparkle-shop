    How fast can a nurse arrive?

    Typically within 60 minutes of booking, depending on your location in Prague and current availability. Same-day bookings are available daily from 8 AM to 8 PM.

    How much does a hangover IV drip cost in Prague?

    Our recommended hangover drip (Nausea Relief) is 2,850 CZK (≈€114), all-inclusive. Alternatives: Electrolyte Reset at 2,950 CZK or Pure Hydrate at 2,450 CZK. Price includes nurse visit, all equipment, and medication.

    Absolutely. All IV drips are administered by registered, licensed nurses using sterile, single-use medical equipment. A prescribing doctor oversees all treatments. We screen every patient before starting.

    Do you come to hotels and Airbnbs?

    Yes — we come to any hotel, Airbnb, apartment, or private home in Prague. We regularly visit all major hotels in Prague 1, Vinohrady, Karlín, and beyond.

    The Nausea Relief drip includes anti-nausea medication, saline hydration, and electrolyte balance. It targets the main hangover symptoms: dehydration, nausea, headache, and fatigue. All ingredients are medical-grade.

    How long does the IV drip take?

    The infusion takes approximately 60 minutes including the health check and setup. Most clients feel significantly better before the drip even finishes.

    Do I need a prescription?

    No — our prescribing doctor handles everything. The consultation and prescription are included in the price. You just book, and we take care of the rest.
